Complaint Management System

Complaint management is the process of handling, managing, responding to, and reporting customer grievances. This process requires a streamlined approach and constant monitoring to ensure faster resolutions. Our Complaint Management System helps users register complaints online, track their status and receive timely resolutions. It improves transparency and efficiency between users and authorities.
